Raising Awareness About Dengue In Singapore

Ng Jia Cheng, Ryan Chai, Tan Wei Jian

About

Singapore saw a huge total number of dengue cases back in 2020 totalling 35,315 with the highest weekly figure of 1787 back in 2020. During the first week of June this year, 1569 cases were reported with a possibility of the number exceeding 2000 sometime in the month (Source).

The government has been actively reminding the citizens living in high risk neighbourhood to keep their environment dengue free by putting up large posters to raise awareness in the neighbourhood.

However, most people are unaware of the cases happening around them in detail, let alone the knowledge of other high risks zones. The proposed solution allows the users to visualise different areasof Singapore that affected by dengue through various idioms for more in-depth insights and awareness.

First, lets take a look at the trend of cases reported over the year. Next, select the bar to view the cases reported weekly throughout the year.
